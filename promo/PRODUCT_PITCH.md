# PRODUCT PITCH — FlegarTech Site

## One-Sentence Pitch

FlegarTech is a polished Next.js 15 portfolio and company site that turns visitors into clients — showcasing projects, services, and case studies with a contact form that actually works.

---

## Product Description

FlegarTech's company site is the front door to a software development studio that builds production-grade web applications. Built on Next.js 15 with TypeScript and Tailwind CSS, it's engineered the same way the client work is — fast, accessible, and maintainable. The site covers everything a prospective client needs to evaluate and reach out: a services overview, a live project portfolio, real case studies, a blog, and a contact form backed by the Resend API.

What separates this from a template portfolio is the craft behind it. Vercel Analytics is wired in from day one, the sitemap is auto-generated for SEO, and every page is fully responsive down to mobile. It's not a placeholder — it's a working product that reflects how FlegarTech actually builds software.

---

## Best 5 Features

- **Working contact form** — Powered by Resend API; messages go directly to the inbox, no third-party form services
- **Project portfolio with case studies** — Real work, real context, not just screenshots behind a hover effect
- **Blog** — Demonstrates expertise and drives organic search traffic
- **Vercel Analytics** — Production traffic monitoring built in from launch
- **Auto-generated sitemap** — SEO-ready without manual maintenance

---

## 30-Second Demo Flow

1. Land on the homepage — immediately see who FlegarTech is and what they build
2. Scroll through the services section to understand the offering
3. Click into the portfolio to browse live project cards
4. Open a case study to read the full build story
5. Navigate to the blog for technical writing
6. Hit the contact page, fill in the form, and send — confirm the form submits successfully

## Target Audience

- Businesses and startups looking to hire a web development studio
- Developers evaluating FlegarTech's technical credibility
- Recruiters or collaborators seeking portfolio evidence
- Anyone referred to FlegarTech who needs a quick trust signal before reaching out
